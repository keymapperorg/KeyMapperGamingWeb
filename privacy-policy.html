This Privacy Policy applies to the Key Mapper Gaming Pro app for mobile devices, together with any related services operated by sds100, collectively referred to as the "Application". sds100 is hereby referred to as the "Service Provider" and acts as the data controller for personal data collected through the Application.

The Service Provider is established in the Netherlands and processes personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 ("GDPR") and other applicable data protection laws.

01 What Information Is Collected

The Application collects the following information when you download and use it. This information is stored locally on your device and is not automatically transmitted to any party.

Information collected locally includes:

  • Your operating system version
  • The properties of any devices connected by a wired, wireless, or Bluetooth connection
  • The properties of any internal or connected devices, such as device model and input capabilities
  • Input events from any internal or connected devices
  • Your key maps and their settings
  • Package names of installed apps used in conjunction with the Application
  • App usage statistics such as session length and navigation within the Application
  • Device and Application logs
Note While input events from internal and connected devices are not themselves personal information, it may be possible to extract personal information from them based on patterns of input events. To avoid collecting this information on your device, it is recommended not to use the Application when entering personal information such as passwords through internal and connected devices.

02 Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

The Service Provider processes personal data only where a lawful basis exists under Article 6 of the GDPR. The applicable legal bases are:

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): When you opt in to sharing anonymous usage data or submit a bug report, you consent to the transmission and processing of that data. You may withdraw your consent at any time.
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)): Processing of crash logs and technical data submitted via bug reports is carried out to maintain and improve the Application. The Service Provider has assessed that this processing does not override your fundamental rights and freedoms.
  • Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b)): Data processed in connection with payment and subscription management (via Google Play Billing and RevenueCat) is necessary to fulfil the purchase or subscription you have entered into.

03 What Information Is Shared

The Application does not share information with any party without your consent.

The Application provides you with the capability to voluntarily share your collected information with the Service Provider in the following ways:

  • When submitting a bug report or feedback within the Application
  • When opting in to sharing anonymous usage data

Upon submitting information to the Service Provider, the information will be transmitted, retained, and used as described in this Privacy Policy.

08 Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right of Access
Request a copy of the personal data the Service Provider holds about you.
Right to Rectification
Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
Right to Erasure
Request deletion of your personal data, subject to certain exceptions under applicable law.
Right to Restriction
Request that the Service Provider restricts processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.
Right to Portability
Request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.
Right to Object
Object to processing based on legitimate interests. Processing will cease unless compelling grounds can be demonstrated.
Right to Withdraw Consent
Where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw it at any time. For opt-in analytics, you can withdraw consent directly within the Application settings, or by contacting the Service Provider. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

To exercise any of these rights, contact the Service Provider at legal@keymapper.app. The Service Provider will respond within 30 days of receipt of your request.

09 Right to Lodge a Complaint

If you believe the Service Provider has not handled your personal data in accordance with applicable law, you have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

The Application is not directed at children under the age of 16. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If you believe a child under 16 has provided personal data through the Application, please contact the Service Provider at legal@keymapper.app so that it can be deleted promptly.

11 Opting Out and Uninstalling

You can stop the collection of information on your mobile device by uninstalling the Application. Uninstalling will prevent the Application from collecting further data, but does not automatically delete information already transmitted to the Service Provider or to third parties.

To opt out of anonymous usage data sharing, you can do so directly within the Application settings at any time. To request deletion of data already transmitted, contact the Service Provider at legal@keymapper.app.
