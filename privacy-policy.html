Privacy Policy

Key Mapper Gaming Pro
Effective date: 8 June 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Key Mapper Gaming Pro app for mobile devices, together with any related services operated by sds100, collectively referred to as the "Application". sds100 is hereby referred to as the "Service Provider" and acts as the data controller for personal data collected through the Application.

The Service Provider is established in the Netherlands and processes personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 ("GDPR") and other applicable data protection laws.

What Information Is Collected

The Application collects the following information when you download and use it. This information is stored locally on your device and is not automatically transmitted to any party.

Information collected locally includes:

  • Your operating system version
  • The properties of any devices connected by a wired, wireless, or Bluetooth connection
  • The properties of any internal or connected devices, such as device model and input capabilities
  • Input events from any internal or connected devices
  • Your key maps and their settings
  • Package names of installed apps used in conjunction with the Application
  • App usage statistics such as session length and navigation within the Application
  • Device and Application logs

Note: While input events from internal and connected devices are not themselves personal information, it may be possible to extract personal information from them based on patterns of input events. To avoid collecting this information on your device, it is recommended not to use the Application when entering personal information such as passwords through internal and connected devices.

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

The Service Provider processes personal data only where a lawful basis exists under Article 6 of the GDPR. The legal bases applicable to this Application are:

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): When you opt in to sharing anonymous usage data or submit a bug report, you consent to the transmission and processing of that data. You may withdraw your consent at any time (see "Your Rights" below).
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)): Processing of crash logs and technical data submitted via bug reports is carried out to maintain and improve the Application. The Service Provider has assessed that this processing does not override your fundamental rights and freedoms.
  • Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b)): Data processed in connection with payment and subscription management (via Google Play Billing and RevenueCat) is necessary to fulfil the purchase or subscription you have entered into.

What Information Is Shared

The Application does not share information with any party without your consent.

The Application provides you with the capability to voluntarily share your collected information with the Service Provider in the following ways:

Upon submitting information to the Service Provider, the information will be transmitted, retained, and used as described in this Privacy Policy.

Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: You may request a copy of the personal data the Service Provider holds about you.
  • Right to rectification: You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to erasure: You may request deletion of your personal data, subject to certain exceptions under applicable law.
  • Right to restriction of processing: You may request that the Service Provider restricts the processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability: You may request a copy of data you have provided to the Service Provider in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: You may object to processing based on legitimate interests. The Service Provider will cease processing unless it can demonstrate compelling legitimate grounds that override your rights.
  • Right to withdraw consent: Where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw it at any time. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal. To exercise any of these rights, contact the Service Provider at legal@keymapper.app. The Service Provider will respond within 30 days of receipt of your request.
